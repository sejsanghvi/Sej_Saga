Experience the captivating world of interactive generative art with Canvas 2D in Vanilla JavaScript. This platform is a unique fusion of technology and creativity, offering users an immersive space for artistic expression and exploration. Whether used for personal expression, therapeutic release, or as a wellspring of inspiration, generative art holds the power to enrich lives and connect us with the limitless potential of the digital realm. Join us and unleash your creativity as we delve into a world where art and technology converge to create meaningful and beautiful experiences.

How to draw?

Step 1: Click on 'Drawing Something' to initiate drawing.

Step 2: Use your mouse to click and move around the canvas.

Step 3: Toggle between shapes, colors, and line widths to tailor your creation.

Features

Draw various shapes including lines, triangles, circles, and squares.

Utilize a spectrum of colors to customize your artwork.

Adjust line width for precise detailing and emphasis.

Save your creations for future viewing and sharing.


Note: The speed of art generation may vary depending on your browser.
