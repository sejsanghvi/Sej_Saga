Threads of Consciousness

Pin & thread, 2023

"Threads of Consciousness" is a participatory art installation that invites individuals to engage in the transformative act of wrapping thread around pins, serving as a conduit to reconnect with their bodies and delve into the depths of consciousness. Through the delicate balance between detachment and engagement, participants navigate the tension inherent in the repetitive motion, transcending mundane routines to explore heightened states of awareness. As the threads intertwine and patterns emerge, the impermanence of the experience underscores the transient nature of existence, offering a contemplative space for introspection and insight into the intricacies of human consciousness.
