DESIGN ART

Hello! I'm Sej Sanghvi, a Visual Designer and Fine Artist based in New York. Currently pursuing an MFA degree at Parsons School of Design, I am deeply passionate about crafting pixel-perfect designs that span brand identities, apps, prototyping, coding, and product management. Whether I'm working with design, code, or a blend of both, I am dedicated to creating user-centric digital solutions that resonate.

As an Artist and Multimedia Designer, I specialize in transforming messages into impactful visual artifacts. With a focus on Visual Communication Design, I bring a unique skill set to each project. With over two years of versatile professional experience, I have collaborated with design agencies and art studios as a consultant, crafting engaging designs for digital and print platforms.

I believe in embracing a captivating fusion of innovation and aesthetic brilliance to create designs and art that leave a lasting impression on our senses and touch the depths of our souls.
