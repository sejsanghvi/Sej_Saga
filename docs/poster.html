To showcase a dynamic poster series crafted for a fitness studio specializing in kickboxing, yoga, and weight training, with the overarching goal of promoting physical activity for enhanced mental health. Each poster distils the essence of its respective activity, using vibrant visuals and thoughtful typography to convey the emotional and physical benefits.

As a kickboxing instructor, I'm passionate about using fitness to enhance mental health, having witnessed the impact of conditions like depression. This poster series aims to inspire individuals to prioritize their well-being through activities like kickboxing, yoga, and weight training, resonating emotionally to encourage action.



I'm Sej Sanghvi, a Visual Designer and Fine Artist, passionate about creating pixel-perfect designs across brand identities, apps, prototyping, coding, and product management. Whether it's through design, code, or a blend of both, I'm committed to creating user-centric digital solutions.
